# app_cancer_risk_assessment
This is a single page HTML tool which can provide cancer risk assessment based on several parameters

Assess your cancer risk now and take corrective actions. 

Now-a-days cancer is spreading very fast. Our best defense for now is awareness and action. 

I have created a tool using Gemini for suggesting which cancer you are in most risk of. This is a single page HTML tool. 

Disclaimer: I have taken some standard benchmark frameworks as reference. But please note that this is not a substitute for professional medical screening and is only for awareness purpose.

Steps:
1. Open the tool by clicking the link https://akhil-jr.github.io/app_cancer_risk_assessment/ 
2. In the tool, the first tab is a summary, and the second tab is the risk assessment.
3. In the second tab, select your inputs from dropdowns and check your cancer risks. This will suggest cancers you need to watch out for.
4. Further, play around with the inputs to see how various parameters affect cancer risks.

Further, 
1. Do your research on how to mitigate it.
2. Take action.
3. Do regular medical tests.

Note: Your inputs are not saved anywhere. If you want maximum privacy, directly download the HTML file https://github.com/akhil-jr/app_cancer_risk_assessment/blob/main/index.html into your laptop or mobile. Double click to open it and do the same process. This way, your inputs will not leave your system.
