Lung & Bronchus

The leading cause of global cancer mortality, overwhelmingly dictated by inhalation of toxicants.

Key Causants

  • Combustible tobacco smoke
  • Indoor radon gas accumulation
  • Asbestos & industrial air pollution

Preventative Actions

  • Strict smoking cessation
  • Radon mitigation in homes
  • Green tea catechins (for non-smokers)

Breast Carcinoma

A complex amalgamation of genetic penetrance and lifetime estrogenic hormone exposure.

Key Causants

  • BRCA1/BRCA2 genetic mutations
  • Obesity (Post-menopausal aromatization)
  • Alcohol consumption & late menopause

Preventative Actions

  • Routine mammographic screening
  • Sustained vigorous physical activity
  • Prolonged breastfeeding (> 6 months)

Colorectal

Gastrointestinal malignancy heavily influenced by diet, inflammation, and microbiome.

Key Causants

  • Red and processed meats (Nitrosamines)
  • Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD)
  • Lynch Syndrome / Familial Polyps

Preventative Actions

  • High dietary fiber (Butyrate production)
  • Long-term NSAIDs / Aspirin use
  • Routine colonoscopy screening

Prostate

Hormonally driven malignancy where aggressive phenotypes are heavily linked to metabolic stress.

Key Causants

  • Family history & Genetics
  • Clinical Obesity (Drives advanced/fatal forms)
  • Advanced age

Preventative Actions

  • Maintenance of low waist-to-hip ratio
  • PSA/DRE screening (risk-stratified)
  • High baseline physical activity

Hepatocellular (Liver)

Biphasic etiology driven by chronic infectious pathogens and severe metabolic toxicity.

Key Causants

  • Hepatitis B & C Viruses
  • Heavy Alcohol (Cirrhosis) & NAFLD/NASH
  • Aflatoxin dietary exposure

Preventative Actions

  • Hepatitis B vaccination & HCV treatment
  • Regular Coffee consumption (highly protective)
  • Weight & alcohol management

Gastric (Stomach)

Mucosal malignancy deeply embedded in bacterial colonization and dietary irritants.

Key Causants

  • Helicobacter pylori infection
  • High salt & traditional pickled foods
  • Processed meats & smoking

Preventative Actions

  • Clinical eradication of H. pylori
  • High intake of fruits/veg (Antioxidants)
  • Limiting dietary sodium

Pancreatic

Highly lethal, rapidly progressing malignancy rooted in metabolic syndrome and toxicity.

Key Causants

  • Type 2 Diabetes & Obesity
  • Tobacco smoking
  • BRCA mutations / Chronic Pancreatitis

Preventative Actions

  • Blood sugar management (prevent hyperinsulinemia)
  • Strict tobacco cessation
  • Maintaining healthy BMI

Esophageal

Split into Squamous (toxin/thermal driven) and Adenocarcinoma (reflux/obesity driven).

Key Causants

  • Obesity + GERD / Barrett's Esophagus
  • Synergy of Alcohol & Tobacco
  • Scalding/very hot beverages

Preventative Actions

  • Management of acid reflux
  • Avoidance of thermal trauma (let drinks cool)
  • Weight management

Cervical

Gynecological malignancy with a near-singular, highly preventable viral etiology.

Key Causants

  • Persistent High-Risk HPV Infection
  • Tobacco smoking (local immunosuppression)

Preventative Actions

  • Prophylactic HPV Vaccination
  • Routine Pap smear / HPV DNA screening
  • Smoking cessation

Melanoma (Skin)

Aggressive malignancy directly resulting from radiation-induced DNA mutagenesis.

Key Causants

  • Ultraviolet (UV) Radiation (Sun/Tanning)
  • Genetic susceptibility (CDKN2A)
  • History of blistering sunburns

Preventative Actions

  • Strict UV avoidance & broad-spectrum barrier
  • Routine dermatological screening

Renal (Kidney)

Urological cancer highly sensitive to metabolic stress and concentrated systemic toxicants.

Key Causants

  • Tobacco smoking
  • Clinical Obesity & Hypertension
  • Industrial chemical exposures

Preventative Actions

  • Smoking cessation
  • Maintaining healthy BMI & blood pressure
